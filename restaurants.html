Restaurants In The Area

1. Mama's Fish House

Mama's Fish House is ont of Maui's most famous restaurants. Family-owned since 1973 and located on Maui's Northshore in beautiful Paia, Mama's stands out from the crowd because the fish are caught and served on the same day. They also share the name of the fisherman and where the fish was caught on their daily menus.

2. Monkeypod Kitchen by Merriman

Monkeypod Kitchen by Merriman has two locations on Maui - one in Wailea and one in Ka'anapali. They also have a spot on Oahu in Ko Olina. The restaurant in Wailea is the original. Their menu is seasonal since they support local, organic farming, ranching, and fishing. They also have live music performed by local musicians.

3. Cafe O'lei at the Mill House

Cafe O'lei is much-loved by Maui locals. Located along Honoapiilani Highway, Waikapu. A lovely spot to get together with friends, reunite with family, or have a business lunch, Cafe O'lei is the best place to be. Food there is so good that they earned a recommendation from Frommer's for their Kihei Location.
