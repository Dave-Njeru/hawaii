Visual & Interaction Design

Poppins is one of the typefaces that has been a popular design tool for building websites. Each letterform is nearly monolinear, with optical corrections applied to stroke joints where necessary to maintain an even typographic color. Quicksand is a sans serif type family of three weights plus matching obliques and a dash vesrion for display and headings. It's a free, open-source typeface with rounded letterforms giving it a warm and friendly appearance.

Images draw attention and trigger emotions. They are an easy way to improve the user experience in a website. 90% percent of all information that we perceive and that gets transmitted to our brains is visual. Buttons with rounded corners are easier on the eyes than a rectangle with sharp edges because they take less cognitive effort to visually process. Placing buttons horizontally shows a well organized and easy to understand web design. Colors have a huge impact upon website users. In building sites, designers often choose green as base color for their pages. Wageningen green is a very versatile color that can be used in a range of designs.
