INTRODUCTION

Hawaii is a volcanic archipelago and an American state. It is located in the Central Pacific Ocean and is made up of several islands. It's about 3,200 KM from the U.S. mainland. It is the only U.S. state outside North America.

FACTOIDS ABOUT HAWAII

1. Invention of Surfing

There is a great deal of evidence that the modern sport of stand up paddle boarding (SUP) was started by the beach boys at Waikiki decades ago. It was revived by big wave suffers on Maui, then introduced to the rest of the world.

2. Largest dormant volcano in the world

Maui's Mount Haleakala highest peak is at 10 feet, and the interior of the summit crater stretches approximately 7.5 by 2.5 miles. Most of this mountain lies below the ocean. It forms 75% of the island.

3. Each island in Hawaii has an official color and flower.

Maui's official color is pink and our flower is Lokelani (aka Damask Rose). The Hawaii state flower is the yellow hibiscus.
